Help

Overlord_exe is a collection of bots and helpers for Tartarus contracts.

How do I interact?

The main way to interact with Overlord_exe is to DM with him.

You can message him:

  • bsky_likes TOKEN GOAL Token is your shareable token from your Tartarus lock and goal is how many likes you need to accumulate to be freed.
  • bsky_selflock Overlord_exe will respond with a link to a form to fill out all the parameters to issue a contract.
